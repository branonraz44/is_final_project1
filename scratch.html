The Committee Got It Wrong

Why BYU Deserved a Spot in the 12-Team Playoff

The 2024-2025 College Football Playoff selection will go down in history as one of the most controversial decisions against the Big 12. Despite an 11-win season, BYU was left out while teams with objectively easier paths walked in.

The Case Against the Field

  1. Strength of Schedule (SOS) Ignored
    • BYU played a grueling Big 12 schedule.
    • Miami's schedule ranked significantly lower (33rd vs. BYU's top tier).
    • Alabama had 3 losses, yet remained in the conversation purely on brand bias.
  2. The "Quality Loss" Fallacy
    • BYU's loss was in a conference championship game.
    • The committee punished BYU for playing an extra game (the 13th game).
  3. The Eye Test vs. Resume
    • Resume: BYU had more Top 25 wins than Miami.
    • Eye Test: The committee claimed they "looked" better, a subjective metric that favors blue bloods.
